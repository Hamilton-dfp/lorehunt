Welcome, Lore Hunter!

Lore Hunt is a collection of essays about the world or Bloodborne that work together to create a comprehensive and cohesive thesis of the story behind the game.

The Lore Hunt thesis is an interpretation of the world of Bloodborne meant for the entertainment of those who like to dwell upon the secrets of the cosmos. There are no claims here of achieving The Eldritch Truth, only an attempt to contemplate these topics on higher planes of thought.
